# TuneMusic

**TuneMusic** is a cloud-based music player designed to help you stream, manage, and enjoy your personal music collection stored in cloud drives and network storage.

With TuneMusic, you can turn your cloud storage into a powerful and beautiful music library.

---

# Features

## ☁️ Cloud Storage Support
TuneMusic allows you to connect multiple cloud storage services and network storage, including:

- SMB (Samba)
- WebDAV
- OneDrive
- Google Drive
- Dropbox
- Baidu Drive
- Aliyun Drive

You can stream your music directly from these services without downloading files locally.

---

## 🎵 Smart Music Organization
TuneMusic automatically organizes your music library by:

- Artists
- Albums
- Playlists

It helps you build a clean and structured music collection from your cloud files.

---

## 📝 Automatic Lyrics Matching
TuneMusic can automatically match and display lyrics for your songs, giving you a better listening experience.

---

## 🎧 Beautiful Music Player
Enjoy a modern and minimal music player interface with:

- Album artwork display
- Playback controls
- Shuffle and repeat
- Background playback

---

## 📂 Build Your Personal Music Library
Your music files stay in your cloud storage while TuneMusic creates a powerful, easy-to-browse music library.

---

# Privacy Policy

TuneMusic respects your privacy.

- TuneMusic does **not upload or store your music files on external servers**.
- All files are streamed directly from the cloud services you connect.
- Your login credentials are only used to authenticate with the selected cloud provider.
- TuneMusic does not sell or share your personal data.

Some metadata and lyrics may be retrieved from third-party services to improve the music experience.
